## About

ToiCF focuses on edge networking, lightweight protocol experiments, and practical validation tools.

Main directions:

- Edge networking and Cloudflare Workers experiments
- Real-link validation and protocol research
- ProxyIP checking, relay testing, and practical runtime diagnostics
- Workers-side proxy prototypes and protocol learning projects

Most repositories here are experimental. They are useful for learning, testing, and runtime verification, but they may not provide stable proxy performance out of the box.

## Principles

- Prefer real runtime verification over surface-level detection.
- Keep experiments small enough to read and reproduce.
- Document limits, failure modes, and practical tradeoffs.
- Separate research prototypes from production-ready claims.
